Mohamed salah - Liverpool Player

Salah was born on June 15, 1992, in Basyoun, Egypt, north of Cairo. As a youth, he idolized such professional players as Ronaldo and Zinedine Zidane, whose moves he tried to emulate during pickup games. At the age of 14 he joined the Egyptian club El Mokawloon's youth team.
